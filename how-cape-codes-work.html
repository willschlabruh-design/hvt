How Cape Codes Work

Everything you need to know about Minecraft cape redemption codes — from purchase to wearing your cape in-game.

What is a cape code?

A single-use alphanumeric code issued by Mojang for official promotional capes. Entering it on the Minecraft website adds that cape to your Microsoft account.

No mods required

Capes are official Minecraft cosmetics. Once redeemed, they appear in your profile on supported platforms — no client mods, resource packs, or third-party launchers needed.

Account-bound

After redemption, the cape is permanently linked to the Microsoft account you used. It cannot be transferred, traded, or moved to another account.

Redemption step-by-step

Follow these steps after you receive your code from HVTDEVIL on Discord.

1

Receive your code

After payment is confirmed in Discord, you receive a unique cape redemption code. Store it securely — each code works once.

2

Sign in on Minecraft.net

Go to minecraft.net/redeem and sign in with the Microsoft account linked to your Minecraft profile.

3

Enter & confirm

Paste your code into the redemption field, submit, and confirm the binding. Mojang will attach the cape to that Microsoft account.

4

Equip in-game

Launch Minecraft on a supported platform, open your profile or character creator, and select the cape. Availability may vary by edition and platform at the time of redemption.

5

Permanent & non-transferable

The cape stays on the account you redeemed on. You cannot undo redemption or move the cape to a different account. Choose the correct Microsoft login before confirming.

Cross-platform note: Many official capes sync to both Bedrock and Java on the same Microsoft account once redeemed, but platform support depends on Mojang’s policies for each cape. Check the official redemption page for your specific item.
Before you redeem: Make sure you are signed into the correct Microsoft account. Redemption is final and cannot be reversed or transferred.
