Refund Policy

How refunds work for digital cape code purchases.

Effective date: June 24, 2026 · See also our Terms of Service

1. Digital goods — all sales final

Cape Codes are digital goods that can be copied or redeemed immediately after delivery. All sales are final unless we expressly agree otherwise in writing in Discord.

2. When we may offer a refund

At our sole discretion, refunds may be considered only when:

  • We fail to deliver a valid Cape Code after confirmed payment;
  • The Cape Code provided was invalid or unusable through no fault of yours, and we cannot provide a working replacement;
  • We cancel your order before the code is delivered.

3. When refunds are not provided

We do not provide refunds when:

  • A valid Cape Code has already been delivered or disclosed to you;
  • You redeemed the code (or it was redeemed on your account);
  • You purchased the wrong cape, used the wrong Microsoft account, or became ineligible to redeem;
  • Payment was sent via PayPal Goods & Services when Friends & Family was required — we may need to refund and ask you to resend correctly, which can delay your order;
  • You initiate a chargeback or payment dispute without contacting us first.

4. How to request help

Contact us on Discord or through our Support form with your order details. Verify you are speaking with getmeintogaming before discussing payments.

5. Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback after receiving a valid code may result in a permanent ban from future purchases and reporting to payment processors. Please contact us first so we can resolve the issue.
